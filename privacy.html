Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Human Architect is operated by Tyson Architect ("we", "us"). This website — human-architect-site.vercel.app — is a marketing page for the Human Architect book system. Our Substack is at humanarchitect8.substack.com.

What data we collect

We collect only what is strictly necessary:

  • Quiz email addresses. If you complete the lifestyle assessment quiz and choose to submit your email, it is sent directly to our email service provider. We do not store it on our own servers.
  • Book purchases. All purchases are processed through Beacons, our e-commerce partner. We do not store your payment details. Beacons handles all transaction data in accordance with their own privacy policy and PCI-DSS standards.
  • Essential cookies only. If you accept cookies, we store a small consent flag in your browser's local storage to remember your choice for 180 days. No analytics cookies, no advertising cookies, no third-party trackers are loaded without your explicit consent.

What we do NOT collect or use

  • No Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel, no third-party advertising SDKs
  • No fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking
  • No sale or sharing of personal data with any third party
  • No analytics or behavioural profiling cookies

How we use your data

  • To deliver the free protocol content you requested via the quiz
  • To send occasional emails about the Human Architect system (only if you opted in)
  • To fulfil book purchases and deliver digital products

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time by clicking the link in any email we send.

Legal basis (UK / EU GDPR)

Our legal basis for processing is consent — you actively choose to submit your email or make a purchase. Article 6(1)(a) UK GDPR.

For purchase processing, the legal basis is contractual necessity — Article 6(1)(b) UK GDPR.

Your rights

If you are in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Rectify any inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (UK) or your local supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us directly. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

Email addresses submitted via the quiz are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion. There is no automated deletion schedule — we rely on your active choice to opt out. Purchase records are retained by Beacons in accordance with their tax and accounting obligations.

International transfers

Our email service provider may process data outside the UK/EU. Where this occurs, appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us via our Substack page at humanarchitect8.substack.com.

We use essential cookies only. No analytics or advertising trackers. Read our privacy policy.
