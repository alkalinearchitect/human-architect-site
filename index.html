The Method

The HALT Protocol: A protocol, not a program.

Programs end. Protocols become you. Every step in the Human Architect method is permanent — once it is integrated, going back feels like decay. This is the HALT Protocol: Honest Audit, Layered Transformation.

Step 01 — Audit

Audit the inputs.

Everything you eat, drink, breathe, see, scroll, and listen to is an input. Inputs become outputs. Inputs become you. Most people audit their bank account but never their bloodwork. The V = P − O formula applies: Value = Potential minus Obstruction. Your potential is fixed. Only the obstruction changes.

Step 02 — Remove

Remove the interference.

Mucus. Parasites. Heavy metals. Seed oils. Bad relationships. Bad media. Bad self-talk. None of these belong in a healthy system. Remove them in that order. This is the Mucusless Diet principle: eliminate what the body cannot use before adding what it needs.

Step 03 — Rebuild

Rebuild the architecture.

Sleep. Sun. Movement. Fresh fruit. Cold water. Hot sauna. Strength training. Time outside. Time without a screen. Daily protocols, until they are no longer protocols — just how you live. This is the Circulation Protocol: blood quality determines everything.

Daily Practice

Foundational protocols.

Start with these. Build the rest on top. These are the 7 core Human Architect protocols for male health and regeneration.

Hydration First

One litre of room-temp lemon water before food. Hydration is the prerequisite for elimination. No coffee until you're hydrated. This is the foundation of the Alkaline Architect protocol.

Tongue Coating Test

Look at your tongue in the morning before water. White, yellow, or fuzzy = the system is obstructed. The tongue is the mirror of digestion. A clean tongue means a clean terrain.

Dry Brushing

Five minutes before showering, dry-brush toward the heart. Lymph stimulation, dead-skin removal, circulation boost. Daily, no exceptions. Part of the Circulation Protocol.

Fruit Before Noon

Fast-digesting fruit only until midday. Easy to digest, energy-dense, eaten in a fasted state. Build the morning around motion and fruit. The Mucusless Diet starts here.
