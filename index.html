Daily Practice

Foundational protocols.

Start with these. Build the rest on top.

Hydration First

One litre of room-temp lemon water before food. Hydration is the prerequisite for elimination. No coffee until you're hydrated.

Tongue Coating Test

Look at your tongue in the morning before water. White, yellow, or fuzzy = the system is obstructed. The tongue is the mirror of digestion.

Dry Brushing

Five minutes before showering, dry-brush toward the heart. Lymph stimulation, dead-skin removal, circulation boost. Daily, no exceptions.

Fruit Before Noon

Fast-digesting fruit only until midday. Easy to digest, energy-dense, eaten in a fasted state. Build the morning around motion and fruit.
