## Top 20 Conversion Problems (ranked by impact on sales)

1. **Unclear Primary Offer & Pricing Friction**  
   No prices, no bundle details, no "what's included in the 5-book system" visible on page. All CTAs point to external Beacons shop without preview of cost or options. Visitor cannot decide to buy without leaving site.

2. **Weak, Repetitive Copywriting**  
   Overuse of "According to the Human Architect system" and "According to Tyson Architect" (feels formulaic, not premium Apple-level). Hero subtext abstract ("body is architecture"). Many sentences long and philosophical instead of painful-problem + specific transformation.

3. **Poor Button Visibility & Mobile Touch Targets**  
   Primary buttons: font-size:10px-11px uppercase, padding 17px 34px but on mobile many collapse. Secondary ".bg" low contrast (paper2 on black). Tiny text in cards ("SECURE THIS BOOK" 9px). No high-contrast, obvious CTAs on mobile.

4. **No Visible Guarantee or Risk Reversal**  
   Only negative "NO REFUNDS ON WEAKNESS" in one spot. No positive 30/60-day guarantee, no "results or protocol support" section. High-ticket feel with zero trust signals for purchase.

5. **Decision Fatigue from Too Many Sections**  
   Hero → Stats → Manifesto → Tools (2 interactives) → Books (5 cards) → Roadmap (4 phases) → Previews (5 panels x6 images) → Founder → FAQ → Final CTA. 10+ sections before buy. Scattered CTAs break focus.

6. **Checkout/Product Friction**  
   No on-site purchase. Every book card opens modal with generic link to same shop URL. No individual pricing, no "buy this book only" option, no cart preview, no urgency (limited bundles?).

7. **Mobile Readability & Hierarchy Failures**  
   Body font 15px ok but labels 8-10px (book meta, tags, footer links). Preview grids on mobile 2-col with tiny labels. Interactive forms in tools have small selects. Long FAQ/inside lists hard to scan on phone.

8. **Inconsistent Brand Tone**  
   Powerful direct lines mixed with repetitive authority phrases and abrupt JS alerts ("Weakness is not an option", "Execute ruthlessly"). Some copy feels preachy or AI-generated instead of cinematic, emotionally powerful, simple.

9. **Lack of Social Proof / Real Credibility**  
   Only invented stats (5 books, 7 protocols). No testimonials, no "X men have run this", no before/after language, no Substack subscriber proof or real results quoted. Founder story short and generic.

10. **Hero CTAs Not Aligned with Conversion**  
    "RUN THE DIAGNOSTIC" good (leads to tools) but second CTA "SECURE THE 5-BOOK SYSTEM" vague – does not communicate bundle value or transformation. No "Get the full system for [price]" .

11. **Interactive Tools Not Monetized**  
    Excellent HALT Diagnostic and V=P-O Calculator engage user but end in alerts or no direct "Buy the recommended book now" button. Lost conversion opportunity after high engagement.

12. **Visual Hierarchy Errors**  
    Stats numbers huge but labels tiny and weak. Book cards have 4 levels of small text above title. Roadmap phases all same weight. Manifesto isolated without follow-up CTA.

13. **Redundant & Slow Preview Section**  
    5 full preview panels with 6 images each (30+ images) add massive scroll length and potential load time. Nice for proof but kills momentum before final CTA.

14. **Weak Founder Section**  
    Uses book image instead of face photo. Story good but no specific results ("from fatigue to clearest version"), no authority (years research, client results, unique methodology proof).

15. **FAQ Misses Objection Handling & CTAs**  
    Good questions but answers lack embedded "buy this" or "start here" links. No "I have porn issue → get Quit PRN" direct paths.

16. **Scattered & Inconsistent CTA Copy**  
    "GET THE SYSTEM", "SECURE THE SYSTEM", "SECURE THE 5-BOOK SYSTEM", "SECURE YOUR COPY", "SECURE THE COMPLETE SYSTEM", "BEGIN YOUR REBUILD". No unified, action-oriented, benefit-driven button language.

17. **Low Emotional Urgency**  
    Strong in places but overall lacks "your best years are being drained RIGHT NOW" repeated pain + "this is your last chance to rebuild before it's too late". Negative framing in one place hurts premium feel.

18. **Roadmap & Phase Interactions Weak**  
    Clicking phases only shows alert popup. No actual content delivery or direct buy path. Feels like demo, not sales tool.

19. **Missing Product Explanation Simplicity**  
    "5 books. 7 protocols. One system." is repeated but never broken into simple "Buy once, get: full detox protocol + retention system + parasite purge + philosophy + 90-day plan."

20. **Technical/Trust Issues**  
    External shop link with no trust badges, no SSL reassurance on site, no email capture before hard sell, no "30-day money back" positive language. Single long HTML file with heavy JS may feel less professional to some.

## Summary of Audit
The site has strong cinematic design, excellent interactive tools, and premium dark aesthetic. However, conversion is crippled by unclear offer, friction to checkout, weak mobile UX, repetitive copy, lack of proof/guarantee, and decision fatigue from overlong page without focused sales path.
